Be Aware. Stay Alert.

  • Never leave your room or office unlocked or hand your room/office key or student/staff card to any unauthorized person.
  • Keep your money and other valuables locked up or out of sight.
  • When moving around campus at night, keep to well-lit walkways or use the security escort services.
  • Do not leave personal belongings such as bags, purses, books, clothing, or other items unattended in public places.
  • Do not leave valuables visible in motor vehicles.
  • Keep your motor vehicle locked and make sure your vehicle has an alarm/immobiliser. Physically check vehicle doors are locked.
  • When off campus, be aware of your surroundings and be on the lookout for opportunistic thieves who may snatch wallets, bracelets, cell phones, and bags. Avoid narrow, quiet walkways and avoid wearing headphones.
  • Avoid displaying valuables when in public, where criminals can see them, such as when waiting at bus stops.
  • There is safety in numbers. Travel and walk in groups of two or more.
  • Know all emergency numbers. These are on the back of your student/staff card.
  • Download the WITS mySOS app.
  • Report any suspicious behaviour in and around campus buildings and in parking areas to Protection Services, and help us keep campus crime-free.
