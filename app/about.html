About us

Overview

Crime is a major concern in South Africa. A lot of individuals fall victim to crime in South Africa . Every South African individual should feel safe regardless of the situation. If only safety was a click away. That is made possible by the Campus-Safety app. individuals are now able to report crime and receive emergency service from a click of a button.

Why Campus Safety was created?

We know that no on follows crime but rather crime follows them and that is why the Campus Safety App was created to assist people who need emergency assistance. We wanted to make it easier for people to be able to call for help. We beilieve that ever individual has a right to safety, and which we make sure that that right is being execised.

Stay in the know

Safety is not only our main concern, but being able to inform society aboy safety related issues as well. Campus Safety App has safety resources, that we would like to call articles. These articles are a way for users to stay informed abou safety related issues. These resource educate the public on topics such as GBV, child abuse, kidnapping and other crime related issues. These articles offer tips and information about these sensetive issues.
